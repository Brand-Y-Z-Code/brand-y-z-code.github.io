About Me

Professional Background Link to heading

I hold a Bachelor of Engineering (B.E.) in Software Engineering from Southwest University, graduating in 2017.

With a solid foundation in computer science and years of professional experience in C++ and Python server-side development, I have developed a keen eye for clean code and efficient system architecture.

My Mission in Education Link to heading

Today, I am transitioning into the field of K-12 Programming Education. I specialize in:

  • Scratch & Python: Building creative games and logical thinking.
  • C++ (Competitive Programming): Helping students master algorithms and data structures.

I believe that programming is the “new literacy” of the 21st century. My goal is to use my engineering background to provide students with a rigorous yet inspiring path to digital mastery.
